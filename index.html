典型语言结构

  • aim
    常见结构：To investigate……; To assess……; To study……; To examine……; To evaluate …… and compare……; To improve……; To describe……; To explore……
  • method
    常见结构：We analyzed……; We studied
  • result
    常见结构：We found……; There was……; ……was (were)……
  • conclusion
    常见结构：This study shows that……; This study suggests that……; This study confirms that……

    Introduction

  • research background
    陈述研究领域，介绍研究背景
  • literature review
    陈述前人研究，已知信息
  • gap
    指出研究必要性
  • research purpose
    说明研究内容，或概述研究活动
  • rationale; academic value
    陈述研究意义和价值
  • orientation; organization
    预告论文框架
